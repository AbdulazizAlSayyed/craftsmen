Proposals

You will receive proposals from freelancers. You can invite specific freelancers to submit proposals. You can also set your job as private, in which case only freelancers you invite can see it.

Milestones

You can create milestones for your job. If you create a milestone, you'll be required to escrow the funds for that milestone. When you approve a milestone, the funds will be released to the freelancer. If a milestone is not approved, you can request a refund of the funds.
